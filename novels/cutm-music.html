Adam & Eve

I had this album in mind for Lem's drive out to the summer beach house.

  • "Intro"
  • "Future Boy"
  • "Delicious"
  • "Broken Nose"  —  An amazing song about dropping your baggage and bad habits and moving forward in life, which is exactly the tipping point Lem was at on his way to the beach house.
  • "Ma Solituda"
  • "Goodbye"  —  This one's a heartbreaker.
  • "For Dreaming"
  • "Outro"  —  Another heartbreaker.

Chrome

I had this album in mind as what Lem would have heard when he put his earbuds in and went running on the beach, listening to the new album his friends just recorded.

  • "Kill Rhythm"  —  In fact, this is the opening track I had in mind for that moment. "The opening riffs of the album exploded into an expanse of sound as I hit the beach running, bringing back a small piece of my mushroom high. The amount of detail in each song was astounding."
  • "I Confess"
  • "Crank"
  • "Broken Head"
  • "Pain" —  This is sort of crazy, but I originally thought the lyrics were "Before the summer faire, I already knew." That one line bloomed in my mind and actually inspired the entire summer faire concept and sequence in Catch Up To Myself. Only years later did I learn that the actual lyrics are "Before the summer fell . . ."
  • "Strange Fruit"
  • "Chrome"
  • "The Nude"
  • "Fripp"
  • "Half Life"
