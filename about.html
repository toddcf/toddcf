About the Author

Todd Croak-Falen graduated from Chapman University with a BFA in Film Production.

In addition to creative writing, he also loves hiking, reading (fiction & nonfiction), music (electronic, 90s alternative, and 90s hip-hop), movies (although TV is way better these days), martial arts (most of all Krav Maga), coding (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), and chess.

He lives in Los Angeles with his wife and daughter.
